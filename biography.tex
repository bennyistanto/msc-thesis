\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Biography}

The author was born in Tuban, East Java, in 1982. He completed primary school 
at SDN Kutorejo 3, Tuban (1988-1994), junior high school at SLTP Negeri 3, Tuban 
(1994-1997), and senior high school at SMU Negeri 1, Tuban (1997-2000). 
He earned his undergraduate degree in Meteorology at Bogor Agricultural University 
(2001-2006). Since 2006, he has worked in the fields of climate analytics and 
geospatial technology at various international organizations. He began his graduate 
studies in Applied Climatology at Bogor Agricultural University in 2022.
